Introduction to R

This is an 8-week course that will teach you to get started with using R Statistical Software for data science and statistical analysis. The course is primarily, but not exclusively, taught using the tidyverse. Prior understanding of introductory statistics and basic programming is helpful, but not required.


The class is adapted from a real course at Cal Poly, a top California university.

All course materials are free and public, and will always remain so.

Do you often find yourself starting to self-teach, and stalling out without the motivation, classmate interaction, and instructor assistance of a more structured class? Then you might want to consider subscribing to the synchronous version of this course!

About Us
